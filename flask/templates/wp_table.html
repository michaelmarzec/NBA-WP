NBA WP% Analysis

Insight into the amount of time an NBA team spends winnning

Glossary

    Winning Time %: Mean age per team
    Losing Time %: Mean age weighted by minutes played per team
    Tie Time %: Mean age weighted by usage rate per team
    Winning %: Mean age of 'Point' position weighted by minutes played per team
    Point Diff: Mean age of 'Combo' position weighted by minutes played per team
    Expected Wins: Mean age of 'Wing' position weighted by minutes played per team
    Expected WP%: Mean age of 'Forward' position weighted by minutes played per team
    Winning Time % vs Winning %: Mean age of 'Big' position weighted by minutes played per team
    Winning Time % vs Expected Winning %: Mean age of 'Big' position weighted by minutes played per team
